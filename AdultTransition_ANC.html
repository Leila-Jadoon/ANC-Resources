Moving On - Transition to Adult Living for Young Adults with Special Needs

A document from the local Family Resource Center describes the local service systems for adults with disabilities

Regional Centers -including our local regional center, Alta California- provide many developmental disability supports in the stat of California, including the paid internship program (see section on employment, below). Indeed, regional center supports can include housing and many other supports, if you can demonstrate that you need these supports- regional center supports are some of the most potentially extensive diability supports in existence. Autistic people without intellectual disabilities are sometimes able to obtain regional center supports under the "fifth category" of service eligibility,but this requires proof of disability-related challenges going back to childhood, whether or not you had a formal autism diagnosis at the time.

The Student Disability Center

requires proof of autism diagnosis to verify that students are autistic. It can be expensive to obtain suitable proof if you do not already have it! Proof is also required by a number of other types of support agencies. Different organizations may have different proof requirements.
